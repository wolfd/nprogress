NProgress.js

A nanoscopic progress bar. Featuring realistic trickle animations to convince your users that something is happening!

NProgress.start() — shows the progress bar — returns progress id

NProgress.set(id, 0.4) — sets a percentage

NProgress.inc(id) — increments by a little

NProgress.done(id) — completes the progress
